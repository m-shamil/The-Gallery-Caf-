OUR STORY

The Gallery Café

The Gallery Café, located in the heart of Colombo,
Sri Lanka, has been a culinary gem for over 5 years.
Established in 2018 by Mr. shamil , the café has
earned a stellar reputation across the country for
its exquisite and flavorful cuisine. Known for
delivering delicious dishes in a warm and inviting
atmosphere, The Gallery Café continues to be a
favorite dining destination for locals and visitors alike.
